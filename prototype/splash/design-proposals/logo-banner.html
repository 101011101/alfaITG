Company Logos Banner — Design Proposals

Three directions for the revolving, bottom-locked partner-logos banner. Each is translucent by default and becomes opaque & paused on hover — hover any band to try it. In production only one is chosen and set to position:fixed; bottom:0.

01

Flat band — fades out near the "Contact us" CTA

A straightforward horizontal strip. A linear-gradient mask dissolves the marquee as it nears the bottom-right corner so logos never collide with the CTA. Lowest-risk, fastest to ship. (In the real app this band is position:fixed; bottom:0; left:0; right:0.)

02

Curved arc carousel — fades on both edges  ★ owner favorite

Logos ride a gentle concave arc, dipping toward the center and rising at the ends. Both left and right edges mask to transparent, so logos materialize and dissolve at the curved tips. Softer and more premium than a flat band.

03

Convex 3D-perspective marquee

The logo track is a tilted plane in CSS 3D space — a conveyor/showroom feel where the center pops forward and the edges recede and fade. Most distinctive, but the most opinionated and the trickiest to keep legible.

Static mockup — pure HTML/CSS with a tiny JS helper that clones the wordmarks for a seamless loop and applies the arc offsets. No external assets.
